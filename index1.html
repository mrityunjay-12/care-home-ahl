Frequently Asked Questions

What services are included in assisted living?
Our assisted living plans cover 24/7 nursing support, daily housekeeping, nutritious meals, laundry services, and recreational activities designed for senior wellness.
Is there a doctor available on campus?
Yes, we have on-call doctors available for regular check-ups and emergencies. We also have a dedicated nursing station functional 24 hours a day.
Can family members visit?
Absolutely. We encourage family visits and have dedicated guest rooms if they wish to stay overnight. Visiting hours are flexible to ensure quality family time.
Do you provide specialized care for dementia patients?
Yes, we offer specialized Dementia Care programs that focus on memory support, safety, and cognitive engagement in a secure environment.
What are the dining options?
We serve freshly prepared, nutritionist-approved vegetarian meals. Special dietary requirements (e.g., diabetic-friendly, low sodium) are catered to with care.
How secure is the facility?
The entire campus is under CCTV surveillance with 24/7 security guards. We also have emergency pull cords in rooms and bathrooms for immediate assistance.
